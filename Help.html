Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers that may help you:

  • How can I make a reservation?
    To make a reservation, you can either book online through our website or call our customer support team.
  • Can I modify or cancel my reservation?
    Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation, depending on the booking policy of your selected room type. Please refer to the terms and conditions during the booking process.
  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept major credit cards and online payment methods. You can find the available payment options during the booking process.
  • Is breakfast included in the room rate?
    The availability of breakfast varies depending on the room type and package you choose. Please check the details while making the reservation.
